Mutual Funds

Mutual funds is a safe way for those who do not want to take the risk of volatility of stock or the debt market. But, the benefits of wealth creation can be availed with the help of our experts at no extra cost.

What?

Mutual Funds are for those who wish to take advantage of returns from stock markets and debt markets but they do not have the time nor soft skills to manage such avenues. The benefits of wealth creation can be availed with help of our expert at no extra cost.

Types

  • Equity plan: 90% of the Amount is invested in equity shares.
  • Debt plan: 80% of the Amount is invested in debt instruments.
  • Equity Hybrid plan: 65% of the Amount is invested in Equity.
  • Debt Hybrid plan: 20% of the Amount is invested in Equity.
Lump Sum, Systematic Investment Plan & Systematic Transfer Plan are some of the ways to invest.
