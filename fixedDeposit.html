Postal Accounts

1. Post Office Time Deposit Account

1.Interest Payable, Rates, Periodicity: Interest is payable annually but it is calculated quarterly.

Years Rate of Interest
1 A/c 6.25%
2 A/c 6.50%
3 A/c 7.25%
5 A/c 7.50%
2.Investment Limits & Denominations: Minimum Rs. 200/- and in multiple thereof. No maximum limit.
3.Salient Features: Account may be opened by individual. 2,3 & 5 year account can be closed after 1 year at discount. Account can also be closed after six months but before one year without interest. The investment under this scheme qualify for the benefit of Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961 from 1.4.2007


2. Post Office Time Deposit Account

1.Interest Payable, Rates, Periodicity: 3.5% per annum on Individual and Joint Accounts.
2.Investment Limits & Denominations: ₹50 to ₹1 lakh for an Individual accounts. And ₹2 lakh for Joint Account
3.Salient Features: Cheque facility is available. The Interest is Tax free.


3. 5 Year Post Office Recurring Deposit Account

1.Interest Payable, Rates, Periodicity: On maturity ₹10 Account fetches ₹728.90. Can be continued for another 5 years on year to year basis. Rate of interest 7.5% (Quarterly Compounded)
2.Investment Limits & Denominations: There is no Limit on Investment. Minimum ₹10 per month or any amount in multiples of ₹5.br> 3.Salient Features: On advance payment of installments for 6 months & one year, rebate is available. Premature withdrawal is allowed after 3 years. No tax is deducted at source.
