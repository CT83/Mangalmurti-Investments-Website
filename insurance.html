Insurance

Something you need but hope never use it.

Life and Non-Life

As per IRDA guidelines it is ideal to have life insurance cover equivalent to 12 times of the earning span of the earning member. There are different kinds of insurance policies according to the needs of the clients. However, insurance should not be mixed with the investments. It is desirable to have low cost \term insurance which can give protection, in case of any eventuality. Generally, insurance premium consists of two elements such as investment and cost of insurance .There are term insurance plans like Amulya Jeevan of L.I.C.which collect only cost of insurance.
We provide services of life insurance.

Infrastructure Bonds

Currently investment upto ₹20,000 in infrastructure bonds is available for additional deduction of income tax rebate over and above the limit of ₹1 lakh. This deduction is available under section 80CCF of the Income Tax Act. These bonds are generally having 10 years period. The interest rate varies from 7.50% to 8.50% p.a. There is a option for exit after 5 years. The interest payment is on annual basis or on maturity.
