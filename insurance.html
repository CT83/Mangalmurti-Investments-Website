Insurance

Something you need but hope never use it.

We provide Life Insurance Policies of LIC.

Life and Non-Life

As per IRDA guidelines it is ideal to have life insurance cover equivalent to 12 times of the earning span of the earning member. There are different kinds of insurance policies according to the needs of the clients. However, insurance should not be mixed with the investments. Generally, insurance premium consists of two elements such as investment and cost of insurance .There are term insurance plans like Amulya Jeevan of L.I.C.which collect only cost of insurance.
We provide all Insurance related services.
