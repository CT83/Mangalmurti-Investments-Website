Postal Schemes

Postal savings systems provide depositors who do not have access to banks, a safe and convenient method to save money.

Public Provident Fund

1.Interest Payable, Rates, Periodicity: 8% per annum
2.Investment Limits & Denominations: ₹500-₹70000 in a financial year. Deposits can be made in lumpsum or in 12 installments.
3.Salient Features: Deposits qualify for deduction from income under Sec. 80C of IT Act. Interest is completely tax-free. Withdrawal is permissible every year from 7th financial year. Loan facility available from 3rd Financial year. The accounts can also be opened in selected Nationalised banks.

Monthly Income Schemes

1.Interest Payable, Rates, Periodicity: 8% per annum ₹80 will be paid every month on a deposit of ₹12000.
2.Investment Limits & Denominations: In multiples of ₹1500, Maximum ₹4.5 lakhs in single account & ₹9 lakhs in joint account.
3.Salient Features: Maturity period is 6 years. Can be prematurely encashed after one year but before 3 years at the discount of 2% of the deposit and after 3 years at the deduction of 1% of the deposit. No tax is deducted at source.
